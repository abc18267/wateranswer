Data confidence and map caveat

Address and ZIP lookups are a starting point, not a legal determination of exposure. Service boundaries, private plumbing, and sampling schedules can change what reaches your tap.

Always confirm with your latest CCR and, when risk is meaningful, a certified lab test from your tap.

How we research

We prioritize primary sources: EPA rules, utility reports, NSF certifications, and named studies.

Methodology and ranking criteria are published in our review policy.
