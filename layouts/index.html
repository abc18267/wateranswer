How we research

Every contaminant page cites actual studies by name and year — no vague attribution. Every filter recommendation references NSF certification data or published removal rates.

We don't get paid to recommend specific products. Affiliate commissions come after testing and ranking, not before. See our review policy.

Where to start

If you have city water, start with Your Water to learn what the EPA requires utilities to test for and what they don't.

If you have a private well, start with Well Water. The EPA doesn't regulate wells. You're responsible for testing.
