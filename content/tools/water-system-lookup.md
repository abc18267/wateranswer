faq:
  - question: "Can ZIP code alone identify my exact water provider?"
    answer: "Not always. Some ZIP codes span multiple systems or include private wells. Treat ZIP lookup as first-pass triage, then verify provider name from your bill or CCR."
  - question: "What if my home is on a private well?"
    answer: "Skip utility lookup and move directly to well testing. Utility CCR data does not represent private well water."
  - question: "When should I test my tap directly?"
    answer: "If contaminants are near limits, if your home has older plumbing, if taste/odor changes suddenly, or if your household includes infants or medically sensitive people."

Use this as your first pass: identify likely utility context, then move to contaminant-specific testing and treatment decisions.

For any higher-risk scenario, verify using your official CCR and a certified tap-water test.
