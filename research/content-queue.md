## CONTENT NOTES & CONSTRAINTS

### YMYL rules apply to all health-adjacent content
- Never say "safe" — use "reduces below EPA limits" or "NSF-certified to reduce X"
- Never say "cures," "eliminates," or "proven" — use "reduces," "may help," "studied"
- showDisclaimer: true on anything touching infant health, pregnancy, cancer, kidney disease, immune suppression
- Emergency callout required for: bacterial contamination, nitrates + infants, boil water advisories

### Affiliate pages need
- affiliateDisclosure: true in front matter (triggers amber box)
- nofollow + sponsored rel attributes on every affiliate link
- Disclosure statement at top (amber box handles this via partial)
- Never claim "we tested" unless literally true. Use "gear we'd genuinely consider using"

### Brand review rules
- Only cite removal percentages from NSF certificates or documented manufacturer lab tests
- Never fabricate removal specs. Link to NSF certification database when possible.
- Berkey: do NOT repeat their PFAS claims without checking current NSF cert status
- Alkaline water: never claim health benefits. Active FTC scrutiny.
- Shower filters: verify chloramine removal against NSF/ANSI 177 per product

### Internal linking requirements
- Every new page links to 2-3 existing pages minimum
- Symptom pages → contaminant hubs → treatment how-tos → product reviews
- Testing pages → test kit reviews
- Hard water spokes → water softener review (once published)
